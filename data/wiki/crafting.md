# Crafting Reference

## Wood Types
Any log variant (oak, spruce, birch, jungle, acacia, dark_oak, mangrove, cherry) yields 4 planks.
Planks of any type can substitute for each other in most crafting recipes.

## Tool Crafting
Pickaxe: 3 material in top row + 2 sticks below center
Axe: L-shape of 3 material + 2 sticks
Shovel: 1 material + 2 sticks
Sword: 2 material stacked + 1 stick
Hoe: 2 material in top-right + 2 sticks

## Basic Recipes
Crafting table: 4 planks in 2x2
Sticks: 2 planks vertically
Furnace: 8 cobblestone in ring
Chest: 8 planks in ring
Torch: 1 coal + 1 stick

## Smelting
Furnace needs 1 fuel item (log=1.5 items, plank=1.5, coal=8, charcoal=8, lava bucket=100).
Smelt raw ore to get ingots. Smelt raw food to cook it.
Blast furnace smelts ores 2x faster. Smoker cooks food 2x faster.

## Brewing
Brewing stand needs blaze powder as fuel.
Start with awkward potion (water bottle + nether wart).
Add secondary ingredient: magma cream=fire resistance, ghast tear=regeneration, spider eye=poison.

## Enchanting
Enchanting table needs lapis lazuli and experience levels.
Bookshelves within 2 blocks increase max enchant level (max 15 bookshelves).
Use an anvil to combine enchanted books onto items.
