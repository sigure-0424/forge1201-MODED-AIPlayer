# Survival Guide

## Hunger
Food bar: 20 points. Natural regeneration starts at 18+, sprint requires 6+.
Best foods: golden carrot (6 saturation), cooked porkchop/beef (12.8 saturation), bread (6).
Eat before hunger drops below 15 to maintain sprint and regeneration.

## The End
Enter via End Portal found in Stronghold (activated with 12 Eye of Enders).
Ender Dragon is the boss — requires bow + arrows and melee weapon.
After defeating dragon, the exit portal opens and dragon egg appears.
End cities are found by throwing an Ender Pearl through the gateway portal.

## The Nether
Enter via Nether Portal (obsidian frame, at least 2x3 inside).
Nether fortress contains blaze spawners (blaze rods for brewing) and nether wart.
Bastions contain piglins — wear gold armor to avoid aggression, barter gold ingots.
Ghasts shoot exploding fireballs — deflect with any item or arrow.

## Farming
Crops: wheat, carrot, potato, beetroot need farmland (tilled with hoe, near water).
Animal breeding: feed two animals of same type their favorite food.
Cow/Sheep: wheat. Pig: carrot/potato. Chicken: seeds. Rabbit: dandelion/carrot.

## Mining
Diamonds found at Y=-54 to Y=-58 (1.18+ generation).
Deepslate starts at Y=0. Ancient debris (netherite) at Y=15 in Nether.
Use Fortune III on diamonds for up to 4 per ore. Silk Touch preserves original block.
