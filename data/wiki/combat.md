# Combat Guide

## Critical Hits
Attack while falling (velocity.y < 0) to deal 1.5x damage (critical hit).
Jump first, wait for the fall phase, then swing. Works with axes and swords.
Axes deal extra damage and can disable shields.

## Shields
Hold a shield in the off-hand and right-click to block.
Shields reduce most melee and projectile damage to near zero.
Axes have a 25% chance to disable (stun) a shield for 5 seconds.
Crouch + use-item activates shield blocking.

## Ender Dragon
The Ender Dragon heals from End Crystals on pillars — destroy all crystals first.
Shoot crystals from a distance with a bow; iron golems can be used as distractions.
During perch phase (dragon sits on the exit portal fountain) attack the head for full damage.
Avoid the dragon breath (purple cloud) — it deals 3 damage per second and lingers.
During flight phase the dragon is immune except at the head; use a bow or wait for perch.
The dragon's breath attack fires when it flies low over the player — dodge sideways.

## Evasion
Strafe sideways to dodge melee swings.
Sprint-jump to close distance quickly.
Use water to break falls (MLG water bucket).
Place a water bucket at your feet when falling from >4 blocks to cancel fall damage.

## Weapons Tier
Netherite > Diamond > Iron > Stone > Wood/Gold
Swords have higher DPS, axes deal more per hit and disable shields.
Bows deal up to 10 damage (power enchantment adds ~25% per level).
